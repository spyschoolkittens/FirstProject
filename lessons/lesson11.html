Animals

Click the picture to learn more about these four fascinating animals!

Cheetahs

Cheetahs are the fastest land animals, estimated to be capable of running at 50 to 80 mph.

Mockingbird

Texas's official state bird is the mockingbird because the species is "a fighter for the protection of his home, falling, if need be, in its defense, like any true Texan."

Wolves

A wolf's hearing is twenty times better than a human's.

Whales

Gray whales make one of the longest annual migrations of any mammal: they travel about 10,000 miles round trip!
